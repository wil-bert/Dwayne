ABOUT ME


I'm a photographer/director based in Kenya. I love taking portraits. It's like my addiction …for which there is no known cure, except to make more. I'm a self-taught photographer. My photography journey started early back in high school when my uncle bought a camera and I got fascinated with it back in 2017. I've been in the game now for approximately 4 years now and currently I work as a freelance photographer. Photographed various artist like Khaligraph Jones, Charisma, YungbeyT etc.
